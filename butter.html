About Butter

Butter is a golden retriever and is currently 8 years old. He is super loyal to my dad and likes to follow him around. Sometimes he will give me some attention as well and I like to think that I am his second favourite in this household. Even though he is the biggest out of the three, he is the most gentle and the small dogs are always the one that will bully him by trying to bite his ears or face. They like to bark at him. He has the nicest smile out of the bunch. What a handsome lad.
